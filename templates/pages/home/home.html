Welcome to Good Hair Daye

good hair dayes await

Good Hair Daye Salon and Extension Bar is a quaint boutique salon tucked away inside L'Enfant Plaza. I specialize in healthy hair, silk presses, and multiple methods of extension installation. With a Paul Mitchell The School Houston education, I bring over 15 years of industry experience and extensive product knowledge for all hair textures. The Extension Bar offers extensions ranging from 12" to 26" that can be matched to any texture from 1A to 4C and custom-colored to blend seamlessly with your natural tresses. Visit the gallery to explore more looks, or stop by the shop to purchase new bundles that are guaranteed to last longer than any you've ever had!
